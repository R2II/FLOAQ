FLOWAQUA 

FlowAqua™ is a nature-tech startup creating a range of superior products that including a wide selection of technical, natural, chemical, botanical & consumable products for a clientele that loves nature. Launched in 2016 based in India & France, FlowAqua™ acts as a holding company for a subset of multiple brands that focus on sustainable and ecological lifestyle products that are developed through analytical insights and deep research. FlowAqua™ operates purely as a B2B supplier and intends to enter the B2C segment by 2020 with a wide range of superior products to be distributed globally.
